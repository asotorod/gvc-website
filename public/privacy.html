GVC Driver App - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

Introduction

GVC School Bus ("we," "our," or "us") operates the GVC Driver mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

When you use the GVC Driver app, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and name provided by your employer for login purposes
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when submitting accident reports (only when you actively submit a report)
  • Photos: Images you choose to attach to accident reports
  • Signatures: Digital signatures for report verification
  • Report Data: Information you enter in accident report forms
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for push notifications

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and store accident reports
  • Verify the location and details of incidents
  • Send you important notifications from dispatch
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improve our services

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • GVC School Bus management and authorized personnel
  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • Insurance companies as needed for incident processing

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information by contacting your supervisor or our support team.

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by GVC School Bus employees only and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
